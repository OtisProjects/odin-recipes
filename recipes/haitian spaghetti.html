Haitian Spaghetti

This Haitian Spaghetti is a flavorful pasta dish made with tomato sauce, diced veggies and chopped hot dogs. It is a common dish served for breakfast in Haiti when eaten alongside hard boiled eggs.

Ingridients

  • Meat
  • Tomato Paste
  • Spaghetti Noodles
  • Veggies
  • Maggi Cubes
  • Haitian Epis

How to Make This Recipe

  1. Step 1: Boil the pasta and set aside. Then add olive oil and epis to a large sauce pan.
  2. Step 2: Saute diced onions, bell peppers, and tomatoes for 5 minutes until the vegetables soften.
  3. Step 3: Add tomato paste, maggi cubes, hot dogs, cloves, and reserved pasta water. Let the mixture simmer for 15 minutes.
  4. Step 4: Add the cooked spaghetti and stir until the sauce coats the noodles. Serve with hard boiled eggs and ketchup on top.
